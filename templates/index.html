From Theory to Practice

As a third-year economics student, I've been fascinated by the intersection of econometric theory and real-world data analysis. This project represents months of dedicated learning and implementation.

The Student Performance Predictor uses advanced ML / Statistical Models to analyze multiple factors affecting academic performance, providing insights that can help Educators and Students optimize learning outcomes.

This isn't just an undergrad statistical project, – it's a practical tool that showcase how economic principles and statistical methods can be applied in practical applications.
